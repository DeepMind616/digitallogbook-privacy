Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Digital Logbook ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Information We Collect

Digital Logbook is designed to work primarily on your device. We collect minimal data:

  • Logbook entries — data you type into the app (stored locally on your device).
  • Device information — basic device type and OS version for crash reporting purposes only.

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any personally identifiable information unless you explicitly provide it.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any data collected is used solely to:

  • Provide and improve the functionality of the app.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues or crashes.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

All logbook entries are stored locally on your device. We do not transmit your entries to any external server unless a cloud sync feature is explicitly enabled by you.

4. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Expo — app build and delivery platform.
  • Google Play Services — for Android distribution.

5. Children's Privacy

Digital Logbook does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided personal data through the app, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data stored on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We encourage you to use device-level security features such as screen locks and encryption.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
